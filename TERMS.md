## 1. The service

Nacre is a mining pool for the Pearl (PRL) blockchain. It aggregates the proof-of-work contributions
of participating miners, submits blocks to the Pearl network, and distributes block rewards to
participants according to the payout scheme they select (PPLNS or Solo).

## 3. Fees

The pool fee is **0%**. Rewards are distributed in full to miners, less only the standard on-chain
network transaction fee required to send a payout.

## 4. Payouts

- Rewards accrue from valid shares you submit and are paid to your mining address.
- Payouts are sent automatically when your balance exceeds the published minimum (currently 1 PRL),
  on the pool's regular payout cycle (currently approximately every 2 hours).
- Payouts depend on the pool actually finding blocks and on those rewards maturing on-chain. Mining
  is probabilistic: **rewards are not guaranteed** and vary with luck, network difficulty, your
  hashrate, and Pearl's block emission.
- Blockchain transactions are irreversible. You are responsible for supplying a correct address.
